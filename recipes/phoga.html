Pho Ga

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat (usually beef), sometimes chicken. Pho is a popular food in Vietnam where it is served in households, street stalls and restaurants countrywide. Pho is considered Vietnam's national dish.

Ingredients

  • 2qt. fresh water
  • 35g cilantro
  • 28g rock sugar
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 1 large white onion, peeled and halved
  • 3" fresh ginger, halved lengthwise
  • 5 star anise
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 3 (3") cinnamon sticks
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1.5 tsp coriander seed
  • 1.5 tsp fennel seed
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp whole coriander
  • 8 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • fine sea salt, to taste

Steps/Instructions

  1. Add ingredients to pressure cooker
  2. Cook for on HIGH for 1 hour
